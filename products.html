Anti-SSEA-3 Antibody (Mouse IgM)

For Dezawa MuseCell® Identification and Isolation (Clone: YKR04)

This mouse monoclonal antibody (Clone: YKR04) specifically recognizes SSEA-3 (Stage-Specific Embryonic Antigen-3), a surface marker of DMC. The antibody demonstrates reliable performance in flow cytometry (FACS), immunostaining (IHC), and magnetic cell separation (MACS).
